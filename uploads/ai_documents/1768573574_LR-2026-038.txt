Legislative No. 2026-038
Strengthening Barangay Governance and Administrative Capacity

WHEREAS, barangays serve as the primary administrative units of the city;

WHEREAS, effective governance requires capable local leadership;

NOW, THEREFORE, this measure is proposed to enhance barangay operations.

SECTION 1. Capacity Development.
Training programs for barangay officials shall be supported.

SECTION 2. Administrative Systems.
Improved record-keeping and reporting systems shall be encouraged.

SECTION 3. Oversight Support.
City offices shall provide technical assistance.

SECTION 4. Performance Monitoring.
Periodic assessments shall be conducted.

SECTION 5. Effectivity.
This measure shall take effect upon approval.
