Legislative No. 2026-039
Expansion of Public Internet Access in Community Facilities

WHEREAS, digital access is essential for education, employment, and public services;

WHEREAS, community facilities can serve as access points for digital services;

NOW, THEREFORE, this measure is proposed to expand public internet access.

SECTION 1. Coverage Areas.
Barangay halls, public libraries, and community centers are covered.

SECTION 2. Infrastructure Support.
Necessary digital infrastructure shall be installed.

SECTION 3. User Access Guidelines.
Policies shall be developed to ensure fair and responsible use.

SECTION 4. System Maintenance.
Regular maintenance shall be ensured.

SECTION 5. Effectivity.
This measure shall take effect upon operational readiness.
